### Changed

- **Breaking:** promoted `Coil` to a first-class arrangement component and
  removed `FoldPattern`; `Accordion` now always alternates adjacent hinge
  directions, and `member_placement` now accepts a `Coil` input alongside
  `Accordion` and `Strip`.
